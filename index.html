🎯 MRON Mod - Warzone 1 revival

MRON Mod was a modification for free cracked Modern Warfare 2019. Started in February 2024 by 2 German enthusiasts, it quickly found a community of OG Warzone fans.

MRON had 2 directions: ✅ Retail (Steam MW) and 🔧 Cracked (1.20 with only Verdansk).

🔥 Key Milestones 🔥

📅 March 2024 — Our great modders were able to launch Modern Warfare matches with Black Ops: Cold War and Vanguard weapons enabled.
📅 June 2024 — We managed to add Rebirth and Fortunes Keep back to official Steam version of Modern Warfare, porting all main modes from old Verdansk's version (not possible on Battle.net).
📅 December 2024 — Version 5.1 Released! — Since the release of Version 5.1, Battle.net is FINALLY SUPPORTED! (Only the AIO, not Rebirth or Verdansk)
